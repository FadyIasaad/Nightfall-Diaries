Background music for Nightfall Diaries videos.

Drop real, royalty-free, cleared music files (.mp3/.wav) here. On every render,
generate_video.py picks ONE at random and mixes it very quietly under the
narration (see MUSIC_BED_VOLUME in config.py). If this folder is empty, the
pipeline falls back to the generated synth ambient bed.

Current tracks (YouTube Audio Library — free & cleared for this channel):
  - Obon Fog (The Mini Vandals)
  - Drifting Memories (The Mini Vandals)
  - Conspiracy Theory (Rod Kim)
